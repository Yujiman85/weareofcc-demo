About Us

Starting off as a small family support service, OFCC has turned into a Companion Care Service committed to serving you. At OFCC, we provide high quality Home Care and Supports services to clients throughout Philadelphia and the surrounding areas. Since 2019, we have provided the resources needed in order to manage and improve the quality of life for all we service. Whether you need daily supports or you are looking for occasional and on-call supports — we are here for you! Our qualified caregivers are here for you and your family to develop and manage a personalized home health plan that’s most convenient for you.
